PROFILE SUMMARY

With over 9 years of experience in the video editing industry, I am a seasoned professional based in the Philippines, specializing in creating compelling visual stories across a variety of platforms. My extensive portfolio includes successful films, TV shows, series, commercials, and digital content, demonstrating my versatility and expertise in the field. I am skilled in transforming raw footage into polished, engaging content that resonates with audiences and meets the highest standards of quality.
